Level 1

Keen Hearing and Smell.In Beastform, Bernard has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing or smell.

Beast Hide.Your Ursilborn skin grants you natural resistance, your AC equals 10 + your Constitution Modifier.

Beastform.As an action, Bernard can transform into any of his available forms. Any equipment Bernard is wearing or carrying isn't transformed. Bernard reverts to his Humanoid form upon falling unconscious or death.

  • Bear Form.Bolstered Strength and Constitution.
  • Hybrid Form.Improved Strength, Dexterity and Constitution.

Stress.Bernard has Stress Points, accumulated when failing a DC10 (+2 for every stress point) Wisdom Save. These saves are required when damaged or aggravated. When all stress Point are accumulated he must spend Stress Points as quickly as possible. A short rest reduces Stress by half while a long rest reduces Stress completely.

  • Wild Shift.When Stress Points are filled, you must shift into Bear form.

Level 2

Martial Arts.Your Unarmed Strikes now deal (1d4 + Strength Modifier) and when you use the Attack Action with an Unarmed Strike on your turn, you can make one Unarmed Strike as your Bonus Action too.

Ki: Way of the Forest.Monks of the Way of the Forest follow their animal instincts, acquiring Blessings that utilise Ki; of which, you have Points. Ki is restored at the end of a short or long rest.

Bernard currently has access to the following blessings of the forest:

  • Illusory Animal.You can produce the illusion of a small forest animal. A successful Intelligence (Investigation) check reveals this to be an illusion. You can have this active to a range of 60ft. walls do not prevent the Illusion, but rely on your memory or assumption of the space. You can cast this at will and can only have one such illusion active at any given time. This requires concentration.
  • Predator.You can spend 1 Ki point to give yourself advantage on the next Dexterity (Stealth) check.
  • Find Path.You can spend 2 Ki points to produce a sensory/visual effect that points in the direction of what you desire for the following 4 hours. This requires concentration.

Monks of the Way of the Forest utilise their animal cunning and intellect to overcome their foes. Some features require your target to make a saving throws against said intellect.

Blessing save DC =
(8 + Proficiency + Intelligence Modifier)

Level 3

Wild Skilled.Your natural instincts sharpen granting proficiency, or if already proficient, gains expertise in 2 of the following: Acrobatics, Intimidation, Investigation, Perception, Stealth and Survival.

Ki: Martial Training.Bernard has mastered a number of Martial Techniques that exact control, ideally resisting the need to transform.

Actions

Unarmed Strike.Melee Weapon Attack: + to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: ( + ) Bludgeoning Damage.

Bite.Melee Weapon Attack: + to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: ( + ) Piercing Damage.

Claw.Melee Weapon Attack: + to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: ( + ) Slashing Damage.

Savage Strike.As an action, you attack the closest creature, friend or foe with an extra 1d6 Damage.


Bonus Actions

Flurry of Blows.Immediately after you take the Attack action on your turn, you can spend 1 ki point to make two unarmed strikes as a bonus action.

Patient Defence.You can spend 1 ki point to take the Dodge action as a bonus action on your turn.

Step of the Wind.You can spend 1 ki point to take the Disengage or Dash action as a bonus action on your turn, and your jump distance is doubled for the turn.

Frenzy.As a bonus action, you enter a frenzy, attacking with advantage and are similarly attacked at advantage until Stress is depleted.

Lash.As a bonus action, you use your Claw Attack at the closest creature, friend or foe.


Reactions

Frightening Aura.As a reaction, anyone who begins their turn within melee range must make a DC Wisdom Save or be frightened of you until the beginning of their next turn.
